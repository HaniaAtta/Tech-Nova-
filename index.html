About Us

Technova is a dynamic and innovative technology company dedicated to bridging the gap between academia and industry. We offer a stimulating environment for aspiring tech enthusiasts to gain practical experience and develop essential skills. Our internships are designed to provide hands-on exposure to real-world projects, mentorship from industry experts, and opportunities to contribute to cutting-edge initiatives.

Mission Statement

To empower the next generation of tech talent by providing world-class internship opportunities and fostering innovation.

Vision Statement

To be the leading technology company renowned for nurturing exceptional talent and driving groundbreaking technological advancements.

Our Services

Technova offers a comprehensive suite of digital solutions to elevate your business. From crafting captivating websites and intuitive mobile apps to unlocking the power of data through insightful analysis, we've got you covered. ForkSight

ForkSight is a mobile app designed with Figma to simplify the lives of health-conscious individuals, especially those juggling busy schedules and dietary restrictions. Our app provides essential tools and resources to support your wellness journey.

Whether you're aiming to lose weight, maintain a balanced diet, or simply live a healthier lifestyle, ForkSight is your go-to companion. With features designed to track your meals, exercise routines, and overall well-being, we empower you to make informed choices and achieve your health goals.

Experience a healthier you with ForkSight.

Key Features:

Meal planning and tracking

Exercise logging

Nutritional information

Personalized recommendations
